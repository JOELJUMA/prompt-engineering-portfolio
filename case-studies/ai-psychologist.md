# AI Psychologist Chatbot – Case Study

##  Problem
Users need a safe space to express emotions and receive helpful, structured responses.

##  Objective
Design prompts that simulate a supportive and intelligent AI psychologist.

##  Prompt Strategy
- Role prompting ("Act as a licensed therapist...")
- Tone control (empathetic, calm, non-judgmental)
- Structured responses (acknowledge → analyze → suggest)

## 💬 Example Prompt
"Act as a professional therapist. A user says: 'I feel lost and unmotivated.' Respond with empathy, identify possible causes, and suggest actionable steps."

##  Output (Example)
- Acknowledges feelings
- Gives structured advice
- Maintains supportive tone

##  Iteration
Initial outputs were too generic → improved by:
- Adding emotional tone instructions
- Structuring response format

## ✅ Result
More human-like, useful, and safe responses
